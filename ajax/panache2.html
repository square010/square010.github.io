Apne Desh Ko Jaano...

IIT Delhi, along with Ministry of Textiles, Govt. of India, hosted a fashion show cum quiz on 16th feb 2014 in TekStyle to help familiarize people with the culture north-eastern states. During the show, Barry O'Brian quizzed the audience on the traditional costumes of the models and the video of culture specific hand crafted products, which were featured in the show.
The footfall for the event was around 3000 people and it was organised in OAT, IIT Delhi. The total budget for the event was around 10-12 lacs. The event was featured in most of the esteemed newspapers like the TOI,the Hindustan Times and many more. It created a huge impact on the textile industry, ministry and students. Textile Engineering Society is dedicated to work for society and come up with many such social and awareness events this year.
