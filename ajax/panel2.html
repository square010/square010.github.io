Panel Discussion

One of the most sought after event is the Panel Discussion, which provides a platform for the young budding minds in the field of Textiles to come and get a wind of what the great minds of the field have to say.
KEY POINTS-
-A panel of delegates from industries namely MR. RAHUL DHARMADHIKARI- Dector, Ahlstrom Fibercomosites India Ltd., MR. MAYANK BINDAL- Vice President, Vardhman Textiles, Ministry of Textiles, Researchers and IIT Deli Alumni.
-Dedicated to guide and motivate students on how to come up with innovative ideas and how to further execute them.
