RULES FOR TECHNICAL TREASURE HUNT

  • Introduction:

    Unlike the common kinds of treasure hunts where we have to collect materials based on hints and clues, we bring you this time an all new adventure with some textile twists.

  • Objective :

    The teams will have to complete the final challenge going through the various stages working on different clues in the given time frame in the labs of Department of Textile Technology

  • Game Procedure:

    The hunt will have some levels. Initially a clue will be given to each of the teams and theyâ€™ll have to find the materials related to the clue in one of the locations which will be described earlier. The next clue will be present with the materials and the game will move on subsequently. Finally they have to bring all the materials to their starting point in the given time constraint. Each material will carry some points and the team with maximum number of points wins.

  • General Rules :

    1. Teams must consist of 3-4 people. People from different departments and colleges can participate provided they carry their valid identity cards.
    2. Only the materials gathered in the given time will be evaluated.
    3. The organizers reserve the right to change the rules as they deem fit.
    4. Certificate will be provided to the winner.
    5. Judges decision will be final and binding to all.
