About tekstyle

'TekStyle', the first of its kind in India, is a national level concursion of students, technologists, industries and delegates from the ministry of textiles to be organised in the month of april 2015. This year Textile Engineering Society IITD brings you a fest in a completely new Indian Avatar evolved from Young Researchers' Symposium (YRS) and Antarang (An Alumni Interaction Event ) which has attracted many textile enthusiasts from many other colleges and industries since 1998. This year we bring you a far better, smarter and bigger platform to display the advancements in textile researches as well as solutions to various industrial problems in the form of different online and offline competitions. We extend a warm welcome to you to be a part of 'TekStyle' and gain from the huge visiblity which only festivals at brand IIT Delhi can provide. This will help you in showcasing your prowess as a huge textile industry by coming forward and contributing to the betterment of all
