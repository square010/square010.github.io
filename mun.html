TekStyle'15

Textile Model United Nations

  • Number of participants per group: 1 or 2
  • Each team will be assigned a country which they will represent. This will be done one day before the event. Each has to present their views about the textile scenario in their country with relevant facts. Each team will be given a chance to speak and this round will be followed by a discussion session, where each team has to discuss and arrive at proposals and policies which will benefit one another. This shall be a friendly platform for the countries to discuss and bring about symbiotic policies for the betterment of their textile Industries and for human welfare.
  • Focus will be on the following but not limited to these criteria:
    1. Facts and relevant data
    2. Major industries
    3. Existing policies within and between countries
    4. Human rights
    5. Presentation skills
