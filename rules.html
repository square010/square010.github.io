RULES FOR POSTER/PAPER PRESENTATION

  1. A team should contain a maximum of 3 members.
  2. Submit an abstract with not more than 200 words, on or before 25th March, 2015.
  3. Send the abstract to tekstyle2015@gmail.com with the subject of format -‘Abstract – your id. no – your team name’.
  4. Please include your complete details (i.e. name, college, course, email id) in the abstract.
  5. The size of the poster should be of 2x3 feet.
  6. The font size should be large enough, so that it can be readable from 1 meter distance.
  7. The font style should be Times New Roman or Calibri.
  8. Your details (i.e. name, id. no, branch) should be placed in the bottom right corner of the poster.
  9. The soft copy of poster (done in Photoshop, PowerPoint or any other tool) should be converted to image format and send to tekstyle2015@gmail.com with subject ‘Poster presentation – your full name’ on or before 1st April, 2015.
  10. The aspects considered in the competition are: Technical content of presenter, Presentation skills, Poster format and appearance.
  11. The paper presentation should last a maximum of 10 minutes. There will be a 5 minutes query and answer session following it.
  12. The oral presentation should be in the form of Microsoft power point slides which contains the brief of your paper.
  13. The recommended fonts are: Title fonts: Times New Roman, 14 font size, Bold and underlined, left alignment; Content fonts: Times New Roman, 12 font size, Justified alignment.
